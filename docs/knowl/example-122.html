Example 2.3.114. Standard MS-PS1-4.

Description: Develop a model that predicts and describes changes in particle motion, temperature, and state of a pure substance when thermal energy is added or removed.

In-class exercise: Talk with students about what happens to forests after wildfires. Talk with students about how charcoal is formed and how it can help with soil health in the burned areas (https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2017/12/171211140412.htm). Discuss the chemical reactions that occur in forming charcoal and the chemical structure of charcoal.

Take home exercise:

Ask students to reflect on how they use ash and charcoal in their daily lives and communities.
