Description: Construct an argument supported by evidence for how increases in human population and per-capita consumption of natural resources impact Earth's systems.

In-class exercise: Talk with students about how fires are being burned in the Amazon for increased food production (https://www.cnn.com/2019/08/23/americas/brazil-beef-amazon-rainforest-fire-intl/index.html). Discuss how these fires threaten the environment, climate, and Indigenous peoples in the Amazon (https://www.theatlantic.com/science/archive/2019/08/amazon-fires-indigenous-lands/596908/).
