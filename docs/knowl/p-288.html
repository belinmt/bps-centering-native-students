In-class and take home exercise: Many Indigenous peoples view animals as our four legged relatives. Concepts of “survival of the fittest” may not be in line with how relatives connect one another. Talk with students about how some animals like wolves (https://www.yellowstone.org/male-gray-wolves-protect-family-members-from-rival-packs/) protect one another despite some individuals having more “weak” traits than others.
