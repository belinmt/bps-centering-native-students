Description: Gather and synthesize information about technologies that have changed the way humans influence the inheritance of desired traits in organisms.

In-class and take home exercise: Talk with students about how Native Americans bred different types of corn. Show videos located here: https://www.allaboutcorn.umn.edu/lessons/corn-breeding. Talk with students about the different varieties of corn and other Native foods (https://www.nrcs.usda.gov/Internet/FSE_DOCUMENTS/nrcs141p2_015559.pdf) and the importance of preserving these foods.
