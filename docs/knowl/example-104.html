Example 2.3.96. Standard MS-LS1-6.

Description: Construct a scientific explanation based on evidence for the role of photosynthesis in the cycling of matter and flow of energy into and out of organisms.

Take home and in-class exercise:

Introduce students to aspen ecology (https://www.fs.fed.us/wildflowers/beauty/aspen/ecology.shtml). Aspen tend to grow near streams and rivers and also in disturbed areas like burned forest areas.

Questions: What traits of the the aspen allow it to reproduce successfully? What is the role of aspen in cycling matter and energy? What is the word for aspen in your Native language? How is aspen used by your community/tribe/Pueblo?
