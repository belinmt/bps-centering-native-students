This context will relate the following standards to climate change and Chaco Canyon, including the history of Chaco Canyon, astronomy and architecture, ties to climate change, and current oil and gas development in the area. Some evidence shows that the peoples who lived in Chaco Canyon may have been impacted by past climate change or a megadrought (http://pastglobalchanges.org/download/docs/magazine/2016-2/PAGESmagazine_2016%282%29_64-65_Betancourt.pdf). More context on Chaco Canyon history can be found here: https://www.nps.gov/chcu/learn/historyculture/index.htm. More information on oil and gas development in the Chaco Canyon region can be found here: https://www.sanjuancitizens.org/chaco.
