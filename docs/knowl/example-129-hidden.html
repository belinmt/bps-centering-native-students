Description: Construct and present arguments using evidence to support the claim that gravitational interactions are attractive and depend on the masses of interacting objects.

In-class exercise: Talk with students about how floods in coastal areas are caused by tides (https://oceanservice.noaa.gov/facts/high-tide-flooding.html). Discuss how tides are caused by gravity from the moon: https://oceanservice.noaa.gov/education/tutorial_tides/tides02_cause.html#:~:text=Gravity%20is%20one%20major%20force,Sumich%2C%20J.L.%2C%201996).

Introduce students to how Indigenous peoples in coastal areas like Australia hold knowledge about tides: http://www.aboriginalastronomy.com.au/content/topics/moon/.
