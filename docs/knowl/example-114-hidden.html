Description: Plan an investigation to determine the relationships among the energy transferred, the type of matter, the mass, and the change in the average kinetic energy of the particles as measured by the temperature of the sample.

In-class exercise: Discuss how thermal energy is transferred during wildfires (https://www.srs.fs.usda.gov/factsheet/pdf/fire-understanding.pdf). Ask students to think about ways their villages or neighborhoods are designed to minimize fire damage or heat transfer.
