Description: Develop and use a model to describe the role of gravity in the motions within galaxies and the solar system.

In-class or take home exercise: Discuss how gravity from giant planets Jupiter and Saturn can change the shape of Earth’s orbit leading to some cycles of climate change. Read more here: https://climate.nasa.gov/news/2948/milankovitch-orbital-cycles-and-their-role-in-earths-climate/

These changes are not the cause of the current climate change which is linked primarily to carbon emissions.

Also discuss how the moon orbits the Earth due to Earth’s gravitational pull and the relative size of the Earth to the Moon.
