In-class: Explore the geologic strata of the San Juan Basin which underlie Chaco Canyon and contain the oil and gas resources that are being extracted. Discuss the ages that range from 40-80 million years of the younger strata lying closer to the surface and the deeper older strata. Discuss the geologic processes that explain how the sediments were deposited.
