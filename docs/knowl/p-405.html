The Species in Peril project was created to bring more awareness to threatened and endangered species and ecosystems both in New Mexico and around the world. Recorded webinars, conference sessions, and other resources are available. Videos of particular interest include the “Flora, Fishes, and Fireflies: Assessing Species in Peril in New Mexico and Abroad” and the “Indigenous Kinship and Multispecies Justice” recorded webinars found at this link (https://speciesinperil.unm.edu/wp/unm-biodiversity-webinar-series-fall-2020-webinar-videos/). Additionally, recorded sessions from the 2017 Decolonizing Nature conference are available here (https://decolonizingnature.unm.edu/videos/).
