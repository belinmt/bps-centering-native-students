Item 2.a.

Students will ask a family member or community member the following questions. Encourage them to reframe the questions in their own words.

  1. What were your favorite places to visit or spend time at throughout your childhood?

  2. Do you have any specific memories?

  3. Are you still able to visit those places? How often?

  4. What changes have you seen in those places?

  5. Have those changes affected you? If so, how?
