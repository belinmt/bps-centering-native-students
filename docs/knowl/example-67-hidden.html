Description: Use models to describe that energy in animals’ food (used for body repair, growth, motion, and to maintain body warmth) was once energy from the sun.

In-class exercise: Relate concepts to farming and gardening practices used to grow food for Pueblo people. Refer to the “Pueblo Farming Methods for your Resilient Garden” webpage and videos embedded on the webpage (https://thegrownetwork.com/pueblo-farming-methods/).
