Description: Read texts and use media to determine patterns in behavior of parents and offspring that help offspring survive.

Suggested classroom activities: Describe how the concept of the “Three Sisters” has been passed down from one generation to another in Native American communities. This knowledge has been passed down from grandparents and parents to children to help them to survive through knowing how to grow these crops together and they are called “sisters” because family help each other to survive.

Questions: How do you and your family help each other? How do your parents and grandparents help you? How will/do you help your younger siblings or children when you grow older?
