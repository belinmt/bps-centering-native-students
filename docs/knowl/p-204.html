Wildfires and floods from monsoon rains are common occurrences in New Mexico. Fire crews from Pueblo communities have assisted in fighting many wildfires in New Mexico and across the West. More recently, Pueblo communities have been helping to restore cultural burning practices to help reduce the occurrences of catastrophic wildfires. Pueblo emergency management and natural resource departments are also working to understand flood risk due to wildfires and intense monsoon storms. Using this context will help students to connect concepts addressed in the following standards to issues Pueblo communities have been active in addressing.
