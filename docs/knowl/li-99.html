Item 1.a.ii.

Have an informal talk about what the students care about most deeply and why. Ideally, include someone from the school or community who is known for being able to engage with students on multiple levels (i.e. Lorilei, someone from Student Success, etc.). This is not necessary though.

  1. Prompts

    1. What are you looking forward to most this season, this school year, in the next 5 years? Why?

    2. Share your favorite memory of something that made you happy. Why did this make you happy?

  2. Or use another preferred method of giving students space to share their values and goals
