Example 2.3.99. Standard MS-LS3-1.

Description: Develop and use a model to describe why structural changes to genes (mutations) located on chromosomes may affect proteins and may result in harmful, beneficial, or neutral effects to the structure and function of the organism.

In-class exercise:

Discuss with students about how contamination, and radioactive contamination in particular, could lead to harmful genetic effects. Talk with students about impacts of contamination of the Rio Grande and other streams from waste from Los Alamos National Laboratory (https://www.latimes.com/archives/la-xpm-2009-nov-01-na-radiation-newmexico1-story.html).
