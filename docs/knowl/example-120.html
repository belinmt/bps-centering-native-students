Example 2.3.112. Standard MS-PS1-2.

Description: Analyze and interpret data on the properties of substances before and after the substances interact to determine if a chemical reaction has occurred.

In-class exercise: Discuss how flame retardants prevent combustion by preventing chemical reactions from occurring (https://www.niehs.nih.gov/health/topics/agents/flame_retardants/index.cfm). Ask students to research the sources of bromine, a commonly used flame retardant.
